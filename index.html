KneeCheck

When pain starts, your plan begins

Knee pain but not sure what to do? KneeCheck helps you understand how urgent your pain is, guides you through safe exercises, and keeps you on track with an AI voice coach and smart motivation. Turn confusion and pain into a clear plan for recovery

Download See Features

FEATURES

guides you

Guided self-diagnosis

KneeCheck leads you through a structured checklist to assess your knee pain step by step. At the end you receive a clear indication whether home care is reasonable or whether you should seek medical help - and how urgent it is.

track symptoms

Tracking pain levels

Log your knee pain in seconds with an intuitive scale and short notes. KneeCheck visualizes your pain history so you and your doctor can see how symptoms evolve over days, weeks, and months.

stay consistent

Motivating gamification

KneeCheck turns your rehab into a small game: earn points, keep streaks, and unlock milestones when you complete your exercises. Gentle reminders and rewards help you stay consistent - even on low-motivation days.

personalized

Personalized exercises

Based on your diagnosis and profile, KneeCheck suggests safe, knee-friendly exercises with clear instructions. Your personal plan adapts over time so you always know what to do today - and what to avoid.

real-time support

AI voice coach

KneeCheck's AI Voice Coach guides you through every exercise in real time. It explains the setup, counts reps, tells you when to hold or rest, and answers your questions like "Am I doing this right?" or "Can we pause?". You can talk to the coach naturally, stay focused on movement instead of your phone, and feel supported throughout every session.
